Understanding Different Body Shapes

Body shapes are a way to describe the human body's proportions and overall appearance. Recognizing different body shapes can help in selecting the right clothing, understanding fitness needs, and appreciating the diversity of human forms. Here, we'll explore some common body shapes and their characteristics.
